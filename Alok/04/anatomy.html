Anatomy Of Webpage

Favicon

The favicon is a small icon displayed in the browser tab. It helps users identify your website easily among other open tabs.

Title

The title appears in the browser's title bar or tab. It provides a brief description of the webpage's content and is crucial for SEO.

Header with Navbar

The header typically contains the top section of the webpage, often including the website's logo, navigation menu, and other important information.

Navbar

The navbar is a navigation bar usually placed horizontally or vertically within the header. It contains links to different sections of the website.

Navlinks

Navlinks are the individual links within the navigation menu (navbar). They point to different pages or sections of the website.

Body/Main Content

The body or main content section is where the primary content of the webpage is displayed. It typically includes various elements such as text, images, videos, and interactive components.

Hero Section

The hero section is a prominent, attention-grabbing section at the top of the webpage. It often includes a large image or video, along with a headline and call-to-action buttons.

Carousel

A carousel is a slideshow component that displays multiple images or other content items in a rotating manner. It is often used to showcase featured content or products.

Sidebar

The sidebar is a vertical column typically placed alongside the main content area. It contains supplementary information, links, or widgets related to the main content.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are blocks of text used to present information in a readable format. They are often used within the main content area to provide explanations, descriptions, or other textual content.

Cards

Cards are versatile content containers used to present information in a visually appealing way. They can include images, text, buttons, and other interactive elements. Cards are often used for displaying articles, products, or user profiles.

Footer

The footer is the bottom section of the webpage. It typically contains copyright information, links to important pages (such as the privacy policy or terms of service), social media icons, and other relevant details.
